Services

I offer a comprehensive range of services in full-stack software development, network engineering, and advanced security solutions to meet diverse business and organizational needs. My software development expertise includes building dynamic web and mobile applications, secure authentication systems, real-time platforms like house booking and delivery apps, and seamless API integrations, utilizing technologies such as Flutter, React, Node.js, TypeScript, and MongoDB to deliver scalable, efficient solutions.

On the network and security side, I provide professional services, including IT infrastructure setup, network installations, firewall configuration (e.g., Sophos), and optimization for performance and security. Additionally, I specialize in designing and implementing cutting-edge CCTV cameras, surveillance systems, and access control solutions in partnership with industry leaders such as Hikvision and Dahua Technology. My commitment is to deliver innovative software, robust IT systems, and reliable security solutions that empower businesses, enhance security, and ensure operational success.

About me

Full-stack Develiper and Network Engineer

I am a highly skilled and versatile Full-Stack Software Developer and Network Engineer with over 4 years of experience delivering innovative, scalable solutions and ensuring the reliability of IT infrastructures. Proficient in a wide range of technologies, I specialize in Flutter for mobile development, React for front-end applications, Node.js for backend services, and MongoDB for efficient database management. My expertise also extends to TypeScript, Express.js, RESTful APIs, and building secure authentication systems. In addition to software development, I have strong competencies in network engineering, including managing and configuring firewalls (such as Sophos), ensuring robust cybersecurity measures, and optimizing IT infrastructure for performance and security. Throughout my career, I have successfully led projects like creating house booking and advertising platforms, implementing real-time rider delivery applications, and developing departmental progress alert systems tailored to business needs. I thrive on solving complex challenges by combining my technical knowledge with a keen analytical mindset. Dedicated to continuous learning, I aim to leverage my diverse skill set to design and implement impactful solutions that enhance user experiences, streamline operations, and drive organizational success
